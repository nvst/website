    Investing as if your values truly matter¶

    Ethical Capital delivers concentrated, high-conviction investment portfolios where ethics come first—not as an afterthought or marketing gimmick. We build portfolios for people who refuse to compromise their values, providing a genuine alternative to both conventional funds and greenwashed ESG products.

    Get Started Learn More

    How We're Different¶

    • Revenue Is Impact

      We select companies that generate positive impact through their core business activities, creating an inseparable link between financial success and ethical outcomes. Most "ethical investments" rely on companies doing good with profits after harmful business activities or optimize for narrow criteria like women on boards—we demand more.

    • Evolving Portfolio Principles

      We treat ethical screening like tending a garden—constantly weeding out problematic companies through ongoing research and adaptation to emerging ethical concerns. Companies find new ways to disappoint us, and our continuous vigilance ensures we don't get stuck with yesterday's ethical standards.

    • Concentration Amplifies Conviction

      Our concentrated approach (<20 companies in our flagship portfolio) enables deep research into each holding, ensuring genuine ethical alignment. Most ethical funds own hundreds of companies, diluting impact and making true ethical assessment impossible. You can print your entire portfolio and understand it in a Sunday afternoon.

    • Beyond Vibes

      We invest in real businesses with strong fundamentals, not just companies that "sound ethical." Many ethical portfolios consist of sci-fi sounding companies with weak fundamentals. Our portfolio often includes unexciting but ethically sound businesses with strong cash flows and real-world impact.

    • Ethics First, Not an Afterthought

      Unlike most firms, we acknowledge the inherent limitations and complexities of ethical investing. We're honest about being "wrong about 15%" of ethical assessments while working diligently to navigate these challenges. Real ethical investing means confronting these limitations, not pretending they don't exist.

    • We Are Not an ESG Fund

      ESG funds often use narrow criteria or superficial metrics to claim ethical status while still investing in companies with problematic practices. We apply rigorous, multi-dimensional ethical screening based on genuine principles, with an exclusion list of nearly 3,000 companies—not box-checking or greenwashing.

    More than just investments¶

    • Ethics-First Investment Management

      Our investment approach starts with rigorous ethical screening as the first step, then applies sophisticated financial analysis to build concentrated portfolios where positive impact and revenue generation are inseparable. We combine bottom-up company research with top-down thematic analysis, structured around four verticals: Infrastructure, Innovation, Lending, and Real Estate.

      Learn about our investment process

    • Cognitive Harmony

      We transform investing from an experience of moral compromise to one of alignment and confidence. Our approach eliminates values-portfolio dissonance and creates peace of mind about ethical alignment. Clients report visible stress reduction during portfolio discussions because there's no need for ethical justifications for holdings.
