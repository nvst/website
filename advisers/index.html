    For Investment Advisers¶

    Partner with Ethical Capital¶

    As an investment adviser, you understand that many clients are increasingly seeking investments that align with their values. Ethical Capital offers a turnkey solution to meet this demand through our comprehensive, ethics-first approach to investing.

    Why Partner with Ethical Capital¶

    Comprehensive Ethical Framework¶

    Rather than developing your own ethical research capability, leverage our robust, unified ethical framework. Our approach is built on thorough research, substantive analysis, and continuous monitoring—giving you a defensible, documented system for ethical investing.

    Clear Value Proposition¶

    Our ethics-first approach provides a distinctive offering that differentiates you from both conventional advisers and superficial ESG options. Our focus on revenue alignment, concentrated portfolios, and active monitoring creates a compelling value proposition for clients seeking authentic ethical investing.

    Transparent, Professional Approach¶

    We maintain a professional investment approach while being transparent about the limitations and challenges of ethical investing. Our honest acknowledgment of being "wrong about 15% of the time" builds credibility and sets realistic expectations.

    Institutional-Grade Documentation¶

    Provide your clients with comprehensive documentation of our ethical framework, screening methodology, and investment rationales. This thorough approach satisfies both regulatory requirements and client expectations for transparency.

    How the Partnership Works¶

    1. Framework Implementation¶

    We provide comprehensive training on our ethical framework and investment approach, enabling you to effectively communicate our methodology to your clients.

    2. Client Onboarding Support¶

    We assist in gathering client values feedback and explaining our unified ethical framework, ensuring clients understand our collective approach rather than expecting personalized ethical screens.

    3. Portfolio Construction¶

    We build concentrated portfolios aligned with our ethical framework, customized for appropriate risk and diversification levels based on your clients' financial needs.

    4. Ongoing Reporting & Communication¶

    We provide regular, thorough reporting on both portfolio composition and ethical alignment, helping you maintain clear communication with your clients.

    5. Ethical Framework Updates¶

    As our framework evolves based on new information and client feedback, we keep you informed of changes and enhancements to our approach.

    Our Approach to Different Client Types¶

    For Values-First Individuals¶

    We help these clients achieve cognitive harmony between their investments and values, eliminating the dissonance that comes from misaligned portfolios. Key messaging: "Understand your entire portfolio in a Sunday afternoon."

    For Disillusioned Skeptics¶

    We offer transparent, substantive ethical investing for clients who've grown cynical after experiencing greenwashing. Key messaging: "We're honest about being wrong 15% of the time."

    For Post-Divestment Activists¶

    We provide a path from avoiding harm to creating positive impact, focusing on revenue alignment and advocacy. Key messaging: "From avoiding harm to creating positive impact."

    For LGBTQ+ Value Aligners¶

    Our intersectional approach recognizes that ethical considerations include queer identity and community concerns. Key messaging: "Investment as an expression of your complete identity."

    For Institutional Clients¶

    Our defensible, documented approach satisfies both ethical alignment and fiduciary duties. Key messaging: "A defensible, documented approach to ethical investing."

    Addressing Client Objections¶

    "I want my portfolio to reflect my specific values"¶

    Our collective approach actually strengthens ethical alignment by incorporating insights from our entire community. Each client's perspective helps enhance our framework, benefiting all clients while maintaining strict ethical standards.

    "What makes this different from ESG funds?"¶

    Unlike ESG funds that retrofit ethics onto conventional portfolios, we build ethics-first portfolios based on revenue alignment. ESG approaches often focus on operational practices rather than the fundamental impacts of a company's products or services.

    "How do you handle disagreements about what's ethical?"¶

    Our ethical framework is collective and evolving. When legitimate ethical concerns arise, we research thoroughly and update all portfolios accordingly. This unified approach means we don't compromise on ethics to accommodate different views—instead, we aim to continuously strengthen our standards.
