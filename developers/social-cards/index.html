    Working with Social Cards¶

    This guide explains how to create and customize social cards for the Ethical Capital website. Social cards are preview images that appear when links to our site are shared on social media platforms like Twitter, LinkedIn, and Facebook.

    Quick Start¶

    For most pages, adding the following front matter will set up a proper social card:

    ---
title: "Your Page Title"
description: "A concise description (1-2 sentences) for social sharing."
coverImage: "image-name.png"  # Optional image from assets/images/
---

    How Social Cards Work¶

    Our site automatically generates social cards for all pages using the MkDocs Material social plugin. These cards follow our brand guidelines and include:

    • The page title
    • A description (from the page's front matter or site default)
    • The Ethical Capital logo and brand colors
    • An optional background image

    Customizing Social Cards¶

    Basic Customization¶

    Add these fields to your page's front matter:

    ---
title: "Page Title"              # Required: Title shown in the social card
description: "Description text"  # Recommended: Short description for social cards
coverImage: "image-name.png"     # Optional: Image from assets/images/
---

    Advanced Customization¶

    For more control over how a specific page's social card appears:

    ---
title: "Page Title"
description: "Description text"
coverImage: "image-name.png"
social:
  cards_layout_options:
    background_image: assets/images/custom-bg.png  # Override background
    background_color: "#3949ab"                   # Override color
    title: "Custom Title for Social Only"         # Override title
    description: "Custom description for social"  # Override description
---

    Recommended Practices¶

    1. Keep titles concise: Aim for titles under 70 characters to avoid truncation
    2. Write compelling descriptions: 1-2 sentences (120-160 characters) that encourage clicks
    3. Use high-quality images: Backgrounds should be at least 1200x630px
    4. Test your cards: Use social media preview tools to verify appearance

    Disabling Social Cards¶

    If you need to disable social cards for a specific page:

    ---
title: "Page Title"
social:
  cards: false
---

    Available Custom Layouts¶

    Our site has these custom social card layouts:

    1. ethical-capital (default): Standard layout with logo, title and description
    2. blog-post: Layout optimized for blog articles with publication date
    3. strategy: Layout designed for strategy pages with a unique visual style

    To specify a layout:

    ---
social:
  cards_layout: blog-post  # Use the blog-post layout
---

    Testing Social Cards¶

    After making changes, you can preview your social cards by:

    1. Building the site with mkdocs build
    2. Checking the generated cards in site/assets/images/social/
    3. Using online preview tools like Social Share Preview

    Technical Details¶

    Our social cards are configured in: - mkdocs.yml: Main social plugin configuration - docs/.social/ethical-capital.yml: Custom layout definition

    For more information, refer to the MkDocs Material documentation.
