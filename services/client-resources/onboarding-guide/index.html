    Welcome to Ethical Capital¶

    We're delighted you've chosen to align your investments with your values. This guide introduces our approach and what you can expect during onboarding.

    Our Commitment to You¶

    At Ethical Capital, we build concentrated portfolios that authentically reflect your values. We focus on where companies make their money, not just how they operate, and maintain vigilant oversight as companies evolve over time. Our intersectional approach goes beyond surface-level vibes to provide substantive alignment you can understand and trust.

    What Makes Us Different¶

    Ethics-First, Always¶

    We are an ethics-first investment firm, not a returns-first firm with ethical considerations tacked on. Investment decisions begin with ethical alignment and then consider the fundamentals, never the reverse.

    Revenue Is Impact¶

    We believe a company's revenue sources are the clearest indicator of its real-world impact. Our analysis focuses on where companies make their money, not just how they operate, because revenue streams reveal a company's true priorities and impacts in the world.

    Concentration Amplifies Conviction¶

    We believe in concentrated portfolios because meaningful ethical alignment requires deep understanding. By focusing on fewer companies, we gain thorough knowledge of each holding, enabling genuine confidence in their alignment with your values.

    What to Expect¶

    Come As You Are Conversations¶

    Every meeting with us begins with a simple question: "What's going on and how can I help?" We believe in meeting you where you are, without judgment or expectations. There's no need to prepare extensively or present a polished financial picture - we're here to understand your authentic needs and concerns.

    Your unique perspective, questions, and values are what make our relationship meaningful. Whether you're just beginning your financial journey or have complex existing investments, we start by listening to you.

    Your Initial Discovery Call¶

    Your first meeting will focus on understanding your values and financial situation. We'll discuss our ethical framework and how it aligns with your priorities, while also determining the appropriate market risk profile for your specific circumstances. Your perspectives will help inform our evolving collective approach.

    Portfolio Understanding¶

    One of our core promises is that you'll be able to understand your entire portfolio in a Sunday afternoon. We ensure you know what you own and why, eliminating the cognitive dissonance that comes from investments misaligned with your values.

    Ongoing Communication¶

    You'll receive regular updates about your portfolio that go beyond numbers to include meaningful discussion of the companies you own, their activities, and how they align with our collective ethical standards. We're honest when we discover we've been wrong (which happens about 15% of the time).

    Your Onboarding Checklist¶

    1. Schedule a Discovery Call
      Use our online calendar to find a time that works for you. Come as you are - we'll start with where you're at.

    2. Complete Your Values Feedback Questionnaire
      After our initial call, we'll send a questionnaire to better understand your perspectives and enrich our ethical framework.

    3. Review Your Investment Approach Document
      We'll create an approach based on our ethical framework and your specific risk profile.

    4. Schedule Your Portfolio Understanding Session
      This session ensures you fully understand our investments and their alignment with our collective values.

    5. Set Up Your Communication Preferences
      Let us know how and when you prefer to receive updates and information.

    Common Questions¶

    "How do you define what's ethical?"¶

    We maintain a unified ethical framework that applies consistently across all client portfolios. Our approach is collective and evolving—when one client raises an ethical issue that others haven't considered, all portfolios are updated to reflect this insight. While we customize market risk and diversification levels to meet individual financial needs, our ethical standards are applied uniformly.

    "What makes you different from ESG funds?"¶

    Unlike ESG funds that typically apply scoring systems to conventional portfolios, we build ethics-first portfolios based on revenue alignment with values. ESG approaches often focus on how companies operate rather than what they actually sell or do.

    "How do you handle companies that change their practices?"¶

    Our evolving portfolio principles approach means we continuously monitor holdings as companies change. When a company shifts in a problematic direction, we reassess and make adjustments accordingly. We're transparent when companies don't meet our expectations.
